Our Team

Meet the Experts

Our team brings together decades of combined experience in LCA, software development, and sustainability assessment.

Chris Mutel

Co-Founder

Chris is the original developer of Brightway, the leading open-source LCA software framework. With a PhD from ETH Zürich in computational methodology of life cycle assessment and nine years as a senior scientist at Paul Scherrer Institute, he brings deep expertise in both the technical and methodological aspects of LCA. His commitment to open science and sustainability is demonstrated through his leadership roles in various initiatives including Départ de Sentier and BONSAI.

João Gonçalves

Co-Founder

João is a software engineer with extensive experience in sustainability-focused development. His background includes leading the software engineering team at ecoinvent and engineering roles at Empa, combining technical expertise with domain knowledge in sustainability assessment. His experience spans both academic and industrial applications, with particular strength in developing robust, user-friendly software solutions.

David Turner

Co-Founder

David is a sustainability expert with a PhD from the University of Southampton and over 12 years of experience bridging science and product development. His background combines academic research in LCA with hands-on experience in carbon accounting startups, including roles at Watershed and Emitwise. His expertise spans both technical aspects of LCA and product management, with particular strength in translating complex environmental data into actionable insights for decision-makers.

Bruno Guimarães

Software Engineer

Bruno is a seasoned software engineer with deep expertise in C++ and systems development. His nearly eight years of experience at Red Hat working on OpenShift demonstrates his capability in developing robust, enterprise-grade software systems. His strong background in computer science and software development brings valuable technical depth to infrastructure and systems architecture.
